About

Hi, I'm a freelance game developer! I currently work as a full-stack web developer for Awesome Inc and Apax Software, where I work directly with clients in need of high-quality websites. I also teach kids how to code at Awesome Inc U. I've worked as a lead instructor of game design where I've used Unity3d to teach object-oriented programming.

I received a Bachelor's in Computer Science from Eastern Kentucky University in the Fall of 2016. My concentration was Interactive Multimedia, which placed a heavy focus on game development. I've been educated and trained for every stage of the development process, including planning/prototyping, pitching, design, programming, testing, presenting, and marketing.

As a freelancer, I'm always looking for cool projects, so don't hesitate to reach out if you're interested! If you happen to find yourself in Lexington, KY, be sure to check out RunJumpDev, a nonprofit dedicated to growing and cultivating the local game development community in Kentucky.

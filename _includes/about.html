About

Hi, I'm a freelance game dev from Kentucky! I like making things that make other people happy. I started a new company, Work-Pending Games, for this very reason. We're working on our first title, and I am filled to the brim with hype. I've worked as a professional software engineer for almost three years, and I also teach kids how to code at Awesome Inc U. By day, I'm a full-stack web developer for Holley Performance Products. By night, I work as the lead designer and lead programmer for Work-Pending Games.

I'm always looking for cool projects, so don't hesitate to reach out if you're interested! I frequently participate in Train Jam and GDC, and try to make it to gaming conferences nearby. If you happen to find yourself in Lexington, KY, be sure to check out RunJumpDev, a nonprofit dedicated to growing and cultivating the local game development community in Kentucky.
